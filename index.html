  • What areas do you provide cleaning services for? ➤

    We provide cleaning services across all major metropolitan areas including [City 1], [City 2], and surrounding suburbs. Contact us to check if we serve your specific location.

  • Are your cleaning products safe? ➤

    Yes, we use eco-friendly, non-toxic cleaning products that are safe for children, pets, and the environment. All our products are certified green and biodegradable.

  • Do I need to be home during the cleaning? ➤

    No, you don't need to be present. We can arrange key pickup or use lockboxes. Our staff is fully vetted and insured for your peace of mind.

  • How long will the cleaning take? ➤

    Cleaning time depends on the size of your space and the package chosen. Typically, a standard apartment takes 2-3 hours, while larger homes may take 4-6 hours.

  • Do you offer one-time or recurring services? ➤

    We offer both! Choose from one-time deep cleaning or regular recurring services (weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly) with discounted rates for recurring plans.

  • Can I customize the cleaning service? ➤

    Absolutely! We offer customizable cleaning packages. You can add services like interior window cleaning, oven deep cleaning, or organization services.

  • What if I'm not satisfied with the cleaning? ➤

    Your satisfaction is guaranteed! If you're not happy with any aspect, contact us within 24 hours and we'll return to re-clean the area at no additional cost.
